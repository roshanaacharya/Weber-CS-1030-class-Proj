Trojan

A Trojan, also known as a Trojan horse, is a type of malware that disguises itself as original software. Trojans can come in differnt format After user is tricked into installing the trojan, the developer gains access to the system, it allows them to steal sensitive data and misuse it for harmful activities.

Trojan can harm the computer of victim in several ways, like it can lead to personal information or bank information theft. It can slow down your system as well as crash the system. And if the developer is quite bad they might even deploy other harmful softwares too. Trojans can do lots of damages but it depends on the intention of the developer.

There are lots of prevention measures that one can take inorder to protect their system, some of them are listed:

  • Avoid downloading stuffs from unknown or untrusted sourceand even if you have to download then do full background checkup before installing.
  • Antivirus can be a great help. You can install antivirus softwares and they will alert you if they find something suspicious.
  • Avoid opening suspicious links or attachments
  • Practice safe browsering habit, just be cautious before visiting unfamilar or weird websites.
  • And always keep your operating system up to date. Like thats one of the high risk of getting trojan in your device.

    • If your system is affected by trojan there are some ways to remedy the situation. First of all separate your system from the network in order to prevent from further damage. Then deep scan your system with the help of antivirus software, it will detect and remove the trojan. If the trojan is not removed completely, you might have to follow some other manual steps or seek the professional help. Obviously, changing all the passwords is very important cause the hacker might have access to those.
