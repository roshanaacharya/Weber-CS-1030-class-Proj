Careers In Computer Science

Software Engineer


Job Description

Software Engineers are responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining software applications or systems. They work on analyzing user needs, designing software solutions, writing code, and testing and debugging software to ensure functionality and performance. They collaborate with cross-functional teams, including product managers and designers, to translate requirements into technical solutions. Software Engineers may specialize in areas such as web development, mobile app development, or embedded systems.

Common Salary $110,140
Education Bachelor's degree in computer science, software engineering.
Experience 5 years of related software engineering experience with a Bachelor's Degree;
3 years of related experience with a Master's Degree;
4 additional years in lieu of a degree.

  • Should have strong programming skills in languages like Java, C++, or Python are important.

Data Engineer


Job description

Data Engineers are responsible for developing, constructing, and maintaining the systems and infrastructure needed to process, store, and analyze large volumes of data. They work on designing and implementing data pipelines, integrating data from various sources, and ensuring data quality and integrity. Data Engineers collaborate with data scientists and analysts to enable effective data-driven decision-making within organizations. They also optimize data infrastructure and implement scalable solutions to handle the growing demands of big data processing.

Common Salary $81,000 to $140,000
Education Bachelor's degree in computer science, information systems
or bachelors degree in related field required for a career as a Data Engineer
Experience 3+ years of experience as a Data Analyst, BI Developer, or Data Scientist
SQL, Data Modeling, Data Documentation or Cataloging;
Creating Dashboards (with tools like Metabase or Sigma).

UX/UI Designer


Job Description

UX/UI (User Experience/User Interface) Designers are responsible for creating intuitive and visually appealing interfaces for websites, mobile applications, and other digital products. They conduct user research to understand user needs and behaviors, develop wireframes and prototypes to outline the user journey and interface layout, and collaborate with development teams to ensure a seamless and user-centric design. UX/UI Designers also conduct usability testing and gather feedback to refine and improve the user experience

Common Salary approx. $85,000
Education Bachelor's degree in computer science
or bachelors degree in related field required for a career as a UI/UX designers.
Experience 2+ years of experience as a Graphics Designer or UI/UX designer.
backgrounds in graphic design, human-computer interaction, or visual arts.
Proficiency in design tools such as Sketch or Adobe XD.

  • Should have solid understanding of user-centered design principles, information architecture, and usability testing.
