My Internet Setup

As I live in University Village, my primary source of accessing the internet is through Eduroam. I couldn't find any router or modem here and I'm unsure about the access points too. So I'll be writing about the Internet setup I used to have at my home back in Nepal.

At home, I used to have a router and modem combination to ensure a reliable internet connection. The router and modem combination I have at home is TP-Link AC1750 Smart WiFi Router which provides the strong signal strength reaches every corner of my house, providing fast and stable internet access for all my devices.

Since the Internet service provider company bought the router for me I didn't buy but If you are interested in getting a similar router, you can find it here.

Talking about Internet service provider, I used to be connected to the internet through Worldlink, one of the fastest-growing internet service providers in Nepal. Worldlink offers high-speed internet with a download speed of 250 Mbps and an upload speed of 300 Mbps, ensuring a seamless online experience. I could effortlessly stream my favorite shows, engage in online gaming, and collaborate on work projects without any lag or interruptions.Even if I had problems arise the support teams used to resolve the issues.
